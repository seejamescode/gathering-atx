About the Gathering ATX

The Gathering ATX has launched on campus at UT Austin by a collective of ATX worship leaders, ministries, students, and friends hosting campus-wide gatherings of worship in the heart of campus.

These unique nights of worship are formed in the hope to make much of the name of Jesus at UT Austin while encouraging and supporting existing ministries and students on campus.
