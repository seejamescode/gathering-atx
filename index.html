A collective of leaders and friends from around Austin hosting pop-up campus-wide gatherings of worship + word in the round at The University of Texas.

The Gathering ATX has launched on campus at UT Austin by a collective of ATX leaders, students, and friends hosting pop-up campus-wide gatherings of worship and word in the round in the heart of campus.

These unique nights of worship are formed in a partnership with a collective of artists and worship leaders from several ministries and churches around Austin, in the hope to make much of the name of Jesus at UT Austin while encouraging and supporting existing ministries and students on campus. Each night is composed "in the round,‚Äù by minimizing a stage and stripping back production, bringing everyone together, and circling back to the heart of worship: a centering on Jesus.

At the heart of The Gathering ATX is the prayer of Jesus in John 17 that all believers would be one. These nights are put together out of the generosity of many friends of Jesus for various moments throughout the semester to worship Jesus, pray for each other and the campus, and at times perhaps even hear briefly from different leaders serving UT Austin and city.
